The program, Venn, assists you in mastering the use of Venn diagrams to evaluate the validity of immediate inferences and syllogisms. The program is named after John Venn, a 19th century logician, who developed this easy diagrammatic method for checking the validity of arguments in traditional class logic.

To learn how to diagram sentences using Venn diagrams, begin with the category "Sentence" on the left of the menu bar at the top of the page. As you master each category, move to the next item to the right on the menu bar.
